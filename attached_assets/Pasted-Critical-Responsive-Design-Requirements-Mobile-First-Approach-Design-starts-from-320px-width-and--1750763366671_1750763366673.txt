RESPONSIVE DESIGN & ALIGNMENT STANDARDS
Critical Responsive Requirements
Mobile Optimization (320px - 768px):

Single column layouts with full-width sections
Stack all elements vertically with proper spacing
Ensure carousel shows 1 product card at a time
Navigation collapses to hamburger menu
Font sizes scale appropriately (minimum 16px for body text)
Touch targets minimum 44px for buttons and links
Eliminate horizontal scrolling completely

Tablet Optimization (768px - 1024px):

Two-column layouts where appropriate
Carousel shows 2 product cards simultaneously
Navigation remains visible with condensed spacing
Maintain touch-friendly interface elements
Optimize image sizes for tablet viewing

Desktop Optimization (1024px+):

Multi-column layouts with generous spacing
Carousel shows 3-4 product cards simultaneously
Full navigation menu with hover states
Desktop-specific interactions (hover effects, larger click targets)
Maximum content width of 1200px with center alignment
